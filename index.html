About

MSc Artificial Intelligence student with research experience in formal methods, efficient machine learning, and search-based AI. My work focuses on developing robust and computationally efficient intelligent systems, combining algorithmic and learning-based approaches. I have applied these methods across domains including knowledge graph reasoning, audio and spatial signal processing, and optimization. I am interested in PhD opportunities in AI where I can contribute to the development of reliable, efficient, and structured intelligent systems.

Research Interests

  • Reliable and Formal AI — Formal verification, logic-based methods, and provably correct AI systems, as well as AI-assisted theorem proving and automated reasoning.
  • Efficient and Sustainable AI — Resource-aware machine learning, including edge AI, low-latency inference, and energy-efficient model design.
  • Search, Planning, and Optimization — Algorithmic and learning-based approaches to search and optimization, including Monte Carlo Tree Search, planning, scheduling, and combinatorial optimization.
  • Audio and Spatial Signal Processing for AI — Machine learning for acoustic data, including sound event detection and localization (SELD), spatial audio modeling, and robust perception under real-world conditions.
  • Structured and Neuro-Symbolic AI — Integrating knowledge graphs, symbolic representations, and learning-based models for structured reasoning and query answering.
